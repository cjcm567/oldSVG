NFA(National Futures Association)

NFA is the industry-wide, self-regulatory organization for the U.S. derivatives industry, providing innovative and effective regulatory programs.

Financial Transactions and Reports Analysis Centre of Canada(FINTRAC)

As Canada’s financial intelligence unit and anti-money laundering and anti-terrorist financing regulator, FINTRAC plays a critical role in helping to combat money laundering, terrorism financing and threats to the security of Canada.

FCA(Financial Conduct Authority)

The Financial Conduct Authority is the conduct regulator for 59,000 financial services firms and financial markets in the UK and the prudential regulator for over 18,000 of those firms.

(MAS)Monetary Authority of Singapore

MAS and Accounting and Corporate Regulatory Authority launched the Variable Capital Companies (VCC) framework today. The VCC is a new corporate structure that can be used for a wide range of investment funds.

(ASIC)Australian Securities&Investment Commission

If you want to run a financial services business, you generally need to be authorised under an AFS licence. An AFS licence authorises you and your representatives to provide financial services to clients.

Cyprus is one of the highly regulated governing bodies in the Forex industry.
