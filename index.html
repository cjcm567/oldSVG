About CJC

We Value More on Quality and not in Quantity.

Why CJC Markets

Our Promises

Our clients choose CJC Markets because we promise:

A friendly and secure trading platform with excellent service and a wide range of products to suit our customers' needs.

01

Superior Execution

We utilize the latest technology in our platform. The platform allows you to use a wide range of different order types. This will give you the benefit of better control of your transactions. At CJC, we are continually looking at ways to improve your trading experience.

02

A Wide Range of Products

CJC provides a wide range of products for its clients. Presently, foreign exchange, precious metals, stock indices and other CFDs are our more popular products. We offer over 100 different currency pairs and many popular stock index and crude oil CFDs.

03

Latest Technology Platform

Our platform encompasses many features and capabilities. It can be used from your desktop by downloading the platform and also through your web browser. It also supports both Microsoft and Apple operating systems and iphone and android applications. We strive to continually upgrade our platform to enhance your trading experience.

04

NDD Trading

With our NDD trading, you will get prices immediately without any dealer intervention. This ensures that you get the best available prices and in the shortest possible time.

05

Competitive spreads and low transaction costs

At CJC, we always strive to provide you with competitive spreads and transaction costs. Our customer will benefit from our low spreads and transaction costs.

06

Eminent Customer Service

We have a team of experienced and dedicated staffs to service you.
